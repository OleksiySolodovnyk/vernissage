ABOUT VERNISSAGE

VERNISSAGE IS A RESTAURANT GROUP THAT USES THE MAGIC OF FOOD TO GIVE UNEMPLOYED YOUNG PEOPLE A CHANCE TO HAVE A BETTER FUTURE.

The group is made up of three restaurants: the flagship Fifteen London established in 2002, Fifteen Amsterdam in December 2004 and Vernissage Cornwall in May 2006.

Vernissage Kyiv is committed to sourcing seasonal, high quality ingredients from the UK and Italy. Fifteen represents the way I would have loved to have been taught myself, it embraces many of the things I love and feel passionate about, not only in the catering industry but also in friendship and family life.

London is full of young people who struggle with traditional schooling, housing, social issues and have no steady work, if any. We are deeply passionate about these young people at restaurant. Vernissage London is committed to sourcingсseasonal, high quality ingredients.

Homemade produce is at the heartbeat of the restaurant, be it fresh pasta, breads, ice cream, a selection of cured meats, cordials, presses and alcoholic drinks such as limoncello, fennel liqueur, almond liqueur and creme liqueur, to name a few.
